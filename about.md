# About

<p class="lead">I am a researcher working on gravitational-wave physics and data analysis for compact binary sources.</p>

My work connects astrophysical interpretation with the practical tools needed to run, monitor, and communicate analyses inside a collaboration. I am especially interested in gravitational-wave lensing searches and the infrastructure that helps researchers move from candidate events to reproducible follow-up results.
