# Research Interests

<p class="lead">I work on a small set of connected problems in gravitational-wave astronomy: targeted searches for lensed signals, low-latency detection and alerts, and sky localization for multi-messenger follow-up.</p>

My research is organized around methods rather than isolated topics. Each area below leads to a dedicated page with a short summary and representative figures.
