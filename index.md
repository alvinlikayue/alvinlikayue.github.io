## Research Focus

<div class="section-grid">
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Low-Latency Detection</h3>
    <p>Real-time gravitational-wave detection systems for rapid identification and follow-up of compact binary events.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Sky Localization</h3>
    <p>Methods for improving localization of gravitational-wave sources and supporting multi-messenger astronomy.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Lensed Gravitational Waves</h3>
    <p>Targeted subthreshold searches, microlensing extensions, and cross-event coherence methods for gravitational-wave lensing.</p>
  </article>
</div>
